FAQ's

What is Kiddy Land?

Kiddy land is a new-age creative platform where we offer covenient and engaging extra curriculum programs in addition to educational programs. Learning new things and making progress helps children improve their self-confidence.

How Many Sessions Will be Held Every Week?

You can choose 2 sessions a week of 1 activity with timings of your choice. If you want to add some extra fun, you can also opt for 2 activities with 4 sessions every week!


We offer courses for 6 Weeks (12 sessions), 12 Weeks (24 sessions), and 24 Weeks (48 sessions). To know more, you can get in touch with our support team!

Are these Sessions Live or Pre-recorded?

All our sessions are conducted live and in real-time by highly trained experts in small batches. The ratio of teacher to learners is 1:4, ensuring that your child can get maximum attention and interactive feedback. This helps your child progressively learn &improve.

How do I book the Demo Class?

Click here to book the Demo class

How do I attend the Demo Class?

Details of the trial session will be sent to you on your registered email ID and WhatsApp number.

Does a Parent Need to be Present during the Demo class?

Our courses are designed to run without any parental support. Only for the trial session, we recommend the parent be present for the last 20 minutes of the session. The instructor will give you feedback about your child's performance in the trial and take you through all the important details of the course. The instructor will also be able to help you answer any questions that you may have about the course or about learning on Astral Academy
